About

Hi! My name is Manu, and I'm a junior full-stack developer and DevOps Engineer. I'm 13 years old, and live in California, USA. My hobbies are coding, playing Minecraft, and watching Star Trek. I have watched the original series, all of the movies, and some of the next generation episodes.

Captain's log stardate 2017.0 (4th grade)
I started my journey in 2017, using scratch to code games. I made mini projects, and started using Weebly and Wix to make websites.

Captain's log stardate 2018.0 (5th grade)
I was tired of wix, and I went on exploring HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to make actual websites. My very first website was about books and was called "ReadQuest".

Captain's log stardate 2020.0 (6th grade)
I continued learning HTML, CSS, and JS, and was having fun exploring GitHub, and Stack Overflow. In late 6th grade (Around the time COVID-19 hit USA), I started learning PHP, and SQL, mainly because I wanted to create a "user account system" for my app. I then realized that this language was fun to learn, and I kept on playing around with it's logic and functions. Around the time school ended, I got an idea of a home inventory app called "KeepInventory". During the summer break, I was coding the entire app. Thank god this year is over.

Captain's log stardate 2021.0 (7th grade)
I have started learning about React, and it's components. It's a very interesting language to learn. I also have learned AJAX in the process. In March, I won my first hackathon called HackOR (see the project "SchoolNerd" for more details) !!! Over the past few months, I have been coding apps, mini apps, and improvising my current ones!

Captain's log stardate unknown. The future is yet to come (lol)
